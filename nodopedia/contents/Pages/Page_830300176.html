Camera-Angle Nodon


You can put the camera in the same position with
the same direction and angle. But just change
the field of view, and the scope of what you're
seeing changes...


Port

Input

None

Output

None

Connection

None

Settings

Camera Field of View

Sets the horizontal angle of the area framed by the camera.
Can be between 10.00 and 120.00. Measured in degrees (°).

Other

You can only call one Camera-Angle Nodon per game.
Also, you can't call up a Game-Screen Nodon or a Camera Nodon when a Camera-Angle Nodon is present.
